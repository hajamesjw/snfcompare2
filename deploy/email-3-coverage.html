5 Weekly Coverage Areas
Policy
CMS Rule Changes & Legislative Action
Medicare and Medicaid reimbursement updates, proposed and final rules, congressional activity affecting SNFs, and state-level regulatory shifts. We track every Federal Register entry and CMS transmittal that touches skilled nursing.
 
Staffing
Workforce Mandates & Labor Market Data
Federal and state minimum staffing requirements, PBJ reporting trends, turnover and vacancy rate analysis, agency utilization patterns, and wage movement across roles. The workforce crisis is the industry's defining challenge — we cover it accordingly.
 
Compliance
Enforcement Actions & Survey Trends
Civil monetary penalties, immediate jeopardy citations, special focus facility designations, state survey trends, and CMS enforcement posture changes. When the regulatory environment shifts, we break down what it means operationally.
 
Finance
M&A Activity & Financial Performance
Acquisitions, divestitures, portfolio transactions, REIT activity, cost report trends, and margin analysis. We track the deals and financial signals that reveal where the industry is heading — and who is positioning for what comes next.
 
Data
CMS Data Releases & Industry Metrics
New Care Compare data drops, Five-Star rating recalculations, PBJ data updates, quality measure changes, and occupancy and census trends. When CMS releases new data, we analyze what changed and what it means for your facility or portfolio.
