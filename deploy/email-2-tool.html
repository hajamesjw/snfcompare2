SNF Compare
Weekly Briefing
 
We built this because the data existed but was nearly impossible to use.
CMS publishes an extraordinary amount of data on every Medicare-certified nursing facility in the country — staffing hours, wage estimates, inspection results, penalty history, quality measures, and ownership records.
The problem? It is scattered across multiple databases, buried in flat files, and presented in formats that were never designed for real-world decision-making.
So we built the SNF Compare Tool — a free, no-login facility comparison engine covering 15,000+ skilled nursing facilities. Two views. One purpose: making the data usable.
Used by administrators, DONs, recruiters, and families researching facilities nationwide.

Try the Family View →
 
What You Can Do
♡
Nurse View
Filter by state and role (NP, RN, LPN, CNA) to compare estimated wages, staffing ratios, turnover rates, and star ratings. Sort by any column to find the facilities that pay and staff the best in your area.
 
☆
Family View
Designed for families evaluating placement options. Compare facilities by overall rating, health inspection score, staffing levels, and quality measures — all in plain language with sortable, filterable tables.
 
✎
Facility Detail Pages
Click any facility name to see its complete profile: staffing hours per resident day, detailed inspection history, penalty amounts, ownership information, quality measure scores, and estimated wage data by role.
 
⇄
Side-by-Side Comparison
Select two or more facilities from the table and compare them head-to-head across every data point — ratings, staffing, wages, inspections, penalties, and quality measures on a single screen.
 
How It Works
Three steps. Under 60 seconds.
1
Choose your view
Select Nurse View for wage and staffing data, or Family View for ratings and quality metrics. Pick your state to narrow the list.
2
Sort, filter, and explore
Click any column header to sort. Use the search bar to find a specific facility. Click a facility name to open its full detail page with inspection history, penalty records, and more.
3
Compare side by side
Select facilities from the table and launch a head-to-head comparison. Every data point — wages, staffing, ratings, inspections — lined up so the differences are immediately clear.
