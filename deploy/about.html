About SNF Compare

Helping families make informed decisions about nursing home care through transparent, accessible data.

Our Mission

Choosing a nursing home for yourself or a loved one is one of the most important healthcare decisions you'll ever make. We created SNF Compare to make that decision easier by transforming complex government data into clear, actionable information.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality healthcare information, regardless of their technical background or familiarity with the healthcare system. Our goal is to empower families with the knowledge they need to find the best possible care.

Our Data Sources

SNF Compare uses publicly available data from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS). Our database includes information on over 15,000 Medicare-certified nursing facilities nationwide.

Data We Include:

  • Five-Star Quality Ratings (overall, health inspections, staffing, quality measures)
  • Staffing levels from Payroll-Based Journal (PBJ) submissions
  • Inspection deficiencies and complaint investigations
  • Quality measures from Minimum Data Set (MDS) assessments
  • Facility characteristics (bed count, ownership, certification)

CMS updates this data monthly. We refresh our database within 1-2 weeks of each release to ensure you have access to the most current information available.
