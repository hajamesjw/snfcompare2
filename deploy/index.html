About SNF Compare

Building transparency in skilled nursing care.

Our Mission

SNF Compare helps families and healthcare professionals make informed decisions about skilled nursing facilities. We believe everyone deserves access to clear, unbiased data when choosing a care facility for a loved one or evaluating a potential workplace.

What We Do

We aggregate publicly available data from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) and present it in a format that is easy to search, compare, and understand. Our tool covers over 15,000 Medicare- and Medicaid-certified nursing facilities across the United States.

Our Data

All facility data displayed on SNF Compare is sourced from official CMS datasets, including:

  • Overall quality star ratings (1–5 stars)
  • Health inspection results and deficiency counts
  • Staffing levels — RN, LPN, and CNA hours per resident per day
  • Quality measure scores for long-stay and short-stay residents
  • Penalty and complaint history

We do not modify, editorialize, or selectively present CMS data. What CMS publishes is what you see.

Who We Serve

Families — Compare quality ratings, inspection histories, and staffing levels to find the best care environment for your loved one.

Healthcare Professionals — Evaluate facilities by staffing ratios, estimated compensation, and turnover rates to find the right workplace.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

SNF Compare is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: If you sign up for early access, we collect your email address. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personal details through our sign-up form.

Automatic information: When you visit our site, we may use your IP address to determine your approximate geographic location (city and state) in order to show relevant nearby facilities. This lookup is performed via a third-party service (ipapi.co) and is not stored on our servers.

Usage data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data such as pages viewed, search queries entered, and facilities compared. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

  • Email addresses are used solely to send early-access invitations and product updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties.
  • IP-based geolocation is used only at page load to personalize facility suggestions. We do not log or retain your IP address.
  • Aggregated usage data helps us improve the tool and understand which features are most useful.

Cookies

SNF Compare does not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. We may use minimal local storage to remember your preferences (such as family vs. professional mode).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • ipapi.co — IP geolocation for location-based facility suggestions. Subject to their privacy policy.

Data Retention

Email addresses collected via the early-access form are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion. To request removal, email hello@snfcompare.com.

Children's Privacy

SNF Compare is not directed at individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
