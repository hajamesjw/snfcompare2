SNF Compare Editorial
January 27, 2026
Money moves
Medicare rates climb 3.7% — the biggest bump in three years
FY2026 payment rule finalized with a $1.4B net spending increase. Wage index recalculations favor rural and mid-market facilities. Meanwhile, Ohio, Florida, and Pennsylvania are pushing Medicaid hikes totaling $1.2B+.
On the radar
The federal staffing mandate clock is ticking
May 2026 deadline for minimum RN and nurse aide hours-per-resident-day. Non-compliance triggers civil monetary penalties and increased survey frequency.
Heads up
OIG puts infection control back in the spotlight
New OIG report found deficiencies in 1 in 3 surveyed facilities — hand hygiene, PPE, and isolation gaps. Expect survey agencies to sharpen focus here.
Deal watch
$820M mega-deal reshapes the Southeast
45 facilities across five states changed hands — the largest SNF acquisition of 2026. Sun Belt consolidation continues as population growth drives demand.
The bottom line

Money flowing in from Medicare and Medicaid, but it comes with strings — tighter staffing mandates, sharper surveys, and a consolidating market that rewards scale.
